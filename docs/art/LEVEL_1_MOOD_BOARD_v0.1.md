## Visual Reference

### Aesthetic Direction
**"Cathedral of the Orb"** — Ancient stone architecture, mysterious but not hostile. The player is a small orb of light navigating massive carved spaces. Scale contrast: tiny player vs. enormous environment.

| Reference | Description | Application |
|---|---|---|
| Dark Souls bonfires | Isolated light source in vast dark space | Torch placement, crystal glow |
| Hollow Knight atmosphere | Ancient ruins, organic architecture | Background shapes, parallax layers |
| Limbo silhouette style | Deep blacks, minimal detail | Far parallax layer 0 |

## Layout Rhythm

### Level 1 Structure
1. **Tutorial start** — safe zone, no hazards, collectibles near
2. **First platform** — introduce jumping
3. **Hazard intro** — single spike tile, easy to avoid
4. **Crystal trail** — rewards along safe path
5. **Gateway** — 70% crystal requirement gate

### Spatial Principles
- Wide platforms at start → narrow at end (progressive challenge)
- Crystals placed to guide path, not punish exploration
- Torches at checkpoints and transition points
- Background layers create sense of scale without blocking gameplay

## Atmosphere Notes
- Not oppressive — torches and crystals provide ambient light
- Player orb is the brightest thing on screen at all times
- Background parallax creates depth without distraction
- Stone walls in `#4a4a6a` / `#1a1a2e` — neutral, ancient
